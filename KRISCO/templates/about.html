About Us

Krisco Sales LLC is a leading distributor of skincare and haircare products from around the world. Founded in 2008, we are proud to have grown into an international buyer and seller of luxury and wholesale goods. Our offices are located in New Jersey and Ontario, Canada, and we have established relationships with over a thousand suppliers from around the world.

Krisco Speciality

We specialize in connecting customers to the best quality products available, and are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest level of customer service. We would love to get to business with you. Visit the contact us page so that we can get started right away.

Our Team

Starting the business back in 2008, we have grown the team to 50 employees and built an incredibly close knit team that celebrates every occasion together. From the strong communication to the collective decisions made, CEO Bob has brought everyone together and fostered a high level of trust.
